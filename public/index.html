// how it works

A 3-step process connecting
travelers and airports

No phone calls, no flying back. Recover your lost item entirely from your phone.

01

Upload your boarding pass

Take a photo of your boarding pass and upload it. Our AI reads your flight details automatically — airport, terminal, flight number, date — and fills in the form for you.

02

We contact all centers simultaneously

kustody sends email inquiries to every relevant lost & found center at your airport at once — airline desks, security checkpoints, terminal offices — 24/7 on your behalf.

03

Confirm, authorize & pay

When your item is found, you'll receive photos and a shipping quote. Pay the delivery fee, sign a digital authorization form through kustody, and send your ID copy directly to the center — your personal data never passes through us.

04

Receive at your door

Once authorized, our courier picks up the item and delivers it to your address — anywhere in the world.

// features

Everything broken about airport
lost & found — fixed.

One request, all centers

Airports have multiple lost & found centers — airline desks, security checkpoints, terminal offices. We contact all of them simultaneously, so you don't have to.

24/7 online submission

No phone calls, no hold music, no language barriers. Submit your claim anytime via our online form and we handle the rest by email on your behalf.

Global shipping

Domestic courier to international express (DHL, FedEx) — handled in one place. We manage customs paperwork too.

Status notifications

Step-by-step updates from submission → confirmation → pickup → delivery → received. No need to follow up yourself.

Shipping insurance

High-value items are insured during transit. Fast compensation in the event of loss or damage on the way to you.

Multilingual support

Available in English, Korean, Japanese, and Chinese. International travelers can use the platform without language barriers.
