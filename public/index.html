// how it works

A 3-step process connecting
travelers and airports

No phone calls, no flying back. Recover your lost item entirely from your phone.

Traveler (item owner)
01

Report your lost item

Submit your airport, flight, and item details in the app. Our AI instantly scans for potential matches.

02

Receive a match alert

When your item is found, you get notified with photos directly from the airport lost & found center.

03

Enter your address & receive

Pay for shipping, confirm your address, and we'll deliver it to your door — anywhere in the world.

Airport lost & found (partner)
01

Log & photograph the item

Scan or photograph found items and register them on the platform. The whole process takes under 30 seconds.

02

Automatic owner matching

The system handles matching with claims. Manage everything from a dashboard — no phone calls needed.

03

Accept pickup & hand over

A kustody courier picks up the item directly from you. No more storage overflow headaches.

Everything broken about airport
lost & found — fixed.

AI-powered matching

Item descriptions and photos are analyzed automatically against the lost & found registry to surface the most accurate matches.

Global shipping

Domestic courier to international express (DHL, FedEx) — handled in one place. We manage customs paperwork too.

Real-time tracking

Step-by-step notifications from claim → match → pickup → delivery → received. Live location tracking included.

Shipping insurance

High-value items are automatically insured during transit. Fast compensation in the event of loss or damage.

Multilingual support

Available in English, Korean, Japanese, and Chinese. International travelers can use the platform without language barriers.

Airport dashboard API

Integrates with existing airport lost & found systems via API. Low implementation cost and fast onboarding.
